# Code of Conduct

CodePrep.AI welcomes respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect differing experience levels and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Sharing private information without permission
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block users who violate these expectations.
